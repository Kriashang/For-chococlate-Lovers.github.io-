FUN FACTS ABOUT CHOCOLATES
  • It takes two to four days to make a single-serving chocolate bar.
  • It takes approximately 400 beans to make 1lb (or 454g) of chocolate!
  • Chocolate was once money that literally grew on trees. In Mayan times, cacao beans were used as currency.
  • It took eight years to develop the recipe for milk chocolate.
  • Chocolates are known to be stress-busters, relaxants and aphrodisiacs.
